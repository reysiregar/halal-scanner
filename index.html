Important Disclaimer

Halal Scanner provides information and guidance about food ingredients based on our extensive database and OCR analysis. However, we cannot guarantee 100% accuracy for several reasons:

  • Manufacturers may change formulations without notice
  • Ingredient sources may vary by region or production batch
  • Islamic scholars may differ in their interpretations
  • Some ingredients require knowledge of their production process

For definitive rulings, we strongly recommend consulting with qualified Islamic scholars or halal certification bodies.

By using Halal Scanner, you acknowledge that results are for informational purposes only.
