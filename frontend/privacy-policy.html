Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect account data such as your name, email address, and encrypted password when you create an account. We also store data you choose to submit, including saved scan results, inaccuracy reports, and testimonials.

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used to provide dashboard features, store your scan history, improve result quality, and respond to reports. OCR and AI are used only for text extraction and cleanup from label images, while halal status decisions are database-driven.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account from the User Dashboard under Privacy and Data. Deleting your account permanently removes your account record and associated dashboard data, including your saved results and reports.

4. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account information. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we continuously improve safeguards where practical.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party infrastructure and API providers for hosting, database, and OCR-related processing. These providers process data only as needed to deliver service functionality.

6. Contact

If you have privacy-related questions, contact us at levinashavila@gmail.com.
